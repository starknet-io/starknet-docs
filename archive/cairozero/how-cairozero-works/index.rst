How Cairo Works
===============

"How Cairo Works" starts from the low-level assembly-like version of Cairo and explains the
syntactic sugar mechanisms applied by the Cairo compiler,
which turns Cairo to a high-level-like language.
If you are looking for a hands-on experience and would like to start writing programs,
take a look at the :ref:`"Hello, Cairo" tutorial <hello_cairo>`.
